About Us

At Kellar Trucking, we began our journey in Kansas, building a reputation for reliability, safety, and personalized service in the trucking industry.

  • Our Foundation: Starting with CDL B operations, we’ve proudly supported local businesses, municipalities, and regional partners with dependable deliveries and distribution services. Our drivers are committed professionals who understand the importance of every load.
  • Branching Out: With our strong Kansas base, we’re now expanding into nearby states, extending our reach while maintaining the same trusted service that has defined us from the beginning. This growth includes branching into CDL A operations, allowing us to handle long-haul freight and larger logistics needs.
  • Our Commitment: Whether serving Kansas or our neighboring states, we deliver with integrity, ensuring freight arrives safely and on time.
  • Our Vision: From local routes to regional expansion, we’re building a company that balances community-focused dependability with broader logistics capabilities. Every mile we drive is guided by our mission: to move freight with care, precision, and trust.
