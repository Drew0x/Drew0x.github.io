About Us

At Kellar Trucking, we began our journey in Kansas, building a reputation for reliability, safety, and personalized service in the trucking industry.

  • Our Foundation: Starting with CDL B operations, we’ve proudly supported local businesses, municipalities, and regional partners with dependable deliveries and distribution services.
  • Branching Out: With our strong Kansas base, we’re expanding into nearby states and moving into CDL A operations for long-haul freight.
  • Our Commitment: Whether serving Kansas or neighboring states, we deliver with integrity, ensuring freight arrives safely and on time.
  • Our Vision: From local routes to regional expansion, we balance community-focused dependability with broader logistics capabilities.
